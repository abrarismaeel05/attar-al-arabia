The Collection

Three original 3D models built in Blender and rendered in Three.js

Oud Noir

A dark, bold take on Arabian Oud. Deep woody notes with black amber and smoky incense, one of the most treasured scents in the Arab world.

View in 3D →

Rose Royale

Based on the famous Damascus Rose from Syria. Rose petals, saffron and musk combined in a round jewel-shaped bottle.

View in 3D →

Amber Essence

Warm amber resin with vanilla and sandalwood in a faceted bottle inspired by the souks of Bahrain. Rich and warm.

View in 3D →

Built with Web 3D Technology

This project uses Three.js and WebGL to display 3D models directly in the browser. Each bottle was modelled in Blender and exported as a GLB file. You can rotate, zoom, animate and toggle wireframe view on each model.
